Buy Cards

The web's best searchable spoiler for Epic TCG now sells singles!

To purchase cards, follow these steps:

  1. Search for the card(s) you want to buy
  2. Click the "Add to Cart" link for your purchase. A Google Checkout shopping cart will appear in the top-right corner of your browser
  3. When you have added all the cards you want to buy, click the "Checkout" link in your shopping cart. You will be redirected to a page where you can enter your shipping and financial information.

Shipping Information

All orders are put into a team bag with a hard plastic sleeve backing, and shipped USPS First Class in a padded envelope.
